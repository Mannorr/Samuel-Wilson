Samuel Wilson is a brand designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. With a sharp eye for visual language and a deep understanding of what makes a brand memorable, I translate ideas into identities that endure.

My process is rooted in strategy — every colour, typeface, and mark is chosen with purpose. I work closely with founders, studios, and organisations who care about the impression they leave.
